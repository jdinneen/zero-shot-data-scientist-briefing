Defensible Claim

The public headline is deliberately narrow: a simple PI plus logistic ranker cleared a hardened zero-shot screen for prioritizing early sampling attention in unseen coastal bacteria basins.

It is a retrospective ranking screen, not a calibrated advisory probability and not a replacement for local monitoring.

Headline Result

Mean AP was 0.1511 against a mean held-out base rate of 0.0909. AP lift is average precision divided by that basin's event rate.

The domain-bootstrap AP-minus-base interval was 0.0365 to 0.086, and all twelve held-out domains beat their own base rate.

Protocol in One Pass

  • Panel contract Rows needed date, site, basin, coordinates, comparable daily bacteria labels, and finite physical-intuition features.
  • Leakage control The headline arm blocked bacteria concentration, hazard labels, exceedance labels, lab-history lags, and target-domain labels.
  • Physical-intuition feature engine Rain, sunlight, tide and flow, wind, date, and location feed label-free formulas that produce PI columns for loading, decay, UV dose, transport, and flushing.
  • Validation Each target basin was hidden, the model trained on the other basins, and the hidden basin was scored without target-domain labels.
  • Gates The freeze required improvement over base rate, permutation nulls, AP-minus-base bootstrap lower bound above zero, and mean-lift lower bound above one.
